The Process
FROM SIGN-UP TO
FIRST LOAD FAST

Getting started with Anchor Dispatch is straightforward. We get you rolling quickly, with zero headaches.

1

Contact Us

Reach out via WhatsApp or email. Tell us about your truck type, preferred lanes, and goals. No commitments yet — just a conversation.

2

Onboarding & Setup

We gather your MC number, insurance, and carrier packet. Our team gets you set up with our preferred brokers and load boards within 24–48 hours.

3

We Find Your Load

Your dedicated dispatcher begins sourcing loads immediately, negotiating top rates, and presenting you with confirmed options that match your preferences.

4

You Drive. We Handle the Rest.

Once you're rolling, we manage all communication with the broker, track your load, handle any issues that arise, and process all your paperwork.

5

Get Paid Fast

Delivery confirmed, documents submitted, invoice sent. With our factoring partners, you can receive payment the same day — or within 24 hours.
